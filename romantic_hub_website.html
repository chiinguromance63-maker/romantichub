I am Romance Chiingu — a 22-year-old Economics graduand at the National Institute of Public Administration (NIPA) in Lusaka, Zambia, graduating September 2026.

I founded Romantic Hub because I experienced firsthand how difficult it is to find quality, affordable, and Zambia-relevant academic support. Most online resources are built for Western students and use examples that do not resonate with what we study here.

Through Clearview Business Solutions, I also provide data analysis and SME consulting to businesses across Zambia — applying the same econometric tools I teach to real-world business problems.
